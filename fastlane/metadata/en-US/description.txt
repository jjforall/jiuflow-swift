JiuFlow is the ultimate app for learning Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) techniques systematically.

【Key Features】

■ Technique Map
Visualize BJJ techniques in an interactive map. Techniques are organized by position, helping you understand the flow between moves.

■ Video Lessons
Detailed instructional videos from professional coaches. Learn subtle movements with slow-motion playback and point-by-point explanations.

■ Dojo Finder
Search for BJJ academies worldwide. Find the perfect gym based on location, level, and atmosphere.

■ Athlete Profiles
Learn about top competitors and their signature techniques. Study match footage alongside technique breakdowns.

■ Progress Tracking
Record techniques you've mastered and visualize your growth. Get personalized recommendations for what to learn next.

【Perfect For】
• Beginners just starting their BJJ journey
• Intermediate practitioners organizing their technique knowledge
• Instructors looking for teaching materials
• Competitors preparing for tournaments

Take your BJJ journey to the next level with JiuFlow.
